About Trivix

We're revolutionizing the music industry by combining blockchain technology with streaming, creating a decentralized platform where artists and fans truly own and control their music experience.

Our Mission

To democratize music ownership and create a fair ecosystem where artists receive fair compensation for their work while giving fans true ownership of the music they love.

Empower artists with direct fan relationships
Give fans ownership and rewards
Build a decentralized music economy

Music Revolution

Our Story

The Idea

Founded in 2024, Trivix was born from the frustration of seeing artists struggle in traditional streaming platforms while fans had no real stake in the music they loved.

The Build

We assembled a team of blockchain experts, music industry veterans, and passionate developers to create a platform that puts power back in the hands of creators and fans.

The Launch

Today, Trivix is live and growing, with thousands of artists and fans already experiencing the future of music streaming and ownership.
