Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Trivix?

Simply sign up for a free account, connect your wallet, and start exploring our vast library of blockchain music. You can earn TRVX tokens by streaming, uploading, and engaging with the platform.

How do artists get paid?

Artists receive direct payments through smart contracts. Every stream, NFT purchase, and engagement generates TRVX tokens that are automatically distributed to the artist's wallet.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use blockchain technology and industry-standard encryption to ensure your data and transactions are secure. We never sell or share your personal information.

Can I withdraw my TRVX tokens?

Absolutely! You can withdraw your TRVX tokens to any supported wallet or exchange them for other cryptocurrencies through our integrated DEX.
